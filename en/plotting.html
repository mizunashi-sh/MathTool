Manual

This tool can draw the graph of an explicit function of one variable \( y=f(x) \) and the surface of an explicit function of two variables \( z=f(x,y) \).

Please follow the hints to fill the form. This toolbox applies math.js to evaluate expressions, and echarts to plot.
