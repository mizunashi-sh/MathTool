Manual

This toolbox calculate the differential through the differences of a point, and applies Romberg method to calculate the integration.

Defaultly, the error limitation is set as \( 1 \times 10^{-6} \). You can modify the setting through the advanced options.

Please follow the placeholder to fill the form. This toolbox applies math.js to evaluate expressions.
