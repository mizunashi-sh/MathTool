  3. Numerical solutions of ODE

Manual

This tool applies the classic Runge-Kutta method to solving initial problems of ODE which fits the format of \( \frac{\mathrm{d}y}{\mathrm{d}x}=f(x,y) \).

Please follow the hints to fill the form. This toolbox applies math.js to evaluate expressions.
